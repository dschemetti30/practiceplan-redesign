About this facility

Richmond Heights High School serves as the central high school within the Richmond Heights Local School District, providing a well-rounded educational experience for students in grades 9–12. Located in Richmond Heights, Ohio, just east of Cleveland, the school brings together a close-knit student population in an environment focused on academic achievement, personal growth, and strong community connection. The campus offers a variety of academic, athletic, and extracurricular opportunities designed to support student success both inside and outside the classroom. From college-preparatory coursework and career-focused programs to competitive athletics and student-led organizations, Richmond Heights High School encourages students to explore their interests, build confidence, and represent the Spartans with pride. With supportive faculty, well-maintained facilities, and a culture centered on excellence, accountability, and community engagement, the school fosters an environment where students are challenged to grow and prepared for future success.

Clear routes, on-site parking, and reliable GPS directions make arrival straightforward for everyday routines and community events.
